# excel-dashboard
📊 Vrinda Store – Annual Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

This Excel dashboard provides a complete annual performance analysis of the Vrinda Store, focusing on customer demographics, sales distribution, and order behavior. It uses Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and interactive slicers to help filter and explore the data effectively.

🚀 Dashboard Overview
The dashboard visually summarizes the store’s performance across multiple business dimensions:

📅 1. Orders vs Sales (Monthly Trend)
This column chart compares:
Number of Orders
Total Sales Amount
It shows how both metrics change month-to-month, helping identify peak sales periods.
Example: February shows a noticeable spike in orders and revenue.

👩‍🦰👨 2. Customer Demographics — Men vs Women
A pie chart highlights the gender distribution of customers.
In this dataset, 100% of purchases were made by women, indicating that the store primarily serves a female customer base.

🚚 3. Order Status Distribution
This pie chart breaks down order outcomes:
Delivered – 90%
Cancelled – 7%
Returned – 3%
This provides insight into order fulfillment performance and return/cancellation behavior.

🌍 4. Sales – Top 10 States
A horizontal bar chart shows the top-performing states by sales volume:
Maharashtra leads with 1860 orders, followed by
Andhra Pradesh (1448)
Karnataka (1430)
Telangana (1112), etc.
This highlights regional demand distribution.

👥 5. Age vs Gender
This chart shows the age category of customers (Adult, Senior, Teenager) along with gender.
The majority of orders come from Adult and Teenager women.
Very few orders come from senior customers.
This helps understand buyer age segments.

🛒 6. Order Channels
A pie chart shows where customers placed their orders (e.g., Myntra, Nalli, others).
In the filtered view shown, 100% of orders come from “Others”, meaning a selected filter is applied.
This chart helps analyze channel performance.

🎛️ 7. Interactive Slicers (Filters)
Users can dynamically filter the entire dashboard using:
Channel
Month
Category
These slicers make the dashboard fully interactive and ideal for decision-making.

✔️ Key Insights

Women are the primary customers for the Vrinda Store.
Most orders are successfully delivered (90%).
Maharashtra is the top-performing state.
Adult and Teenager age groups drive the majority of sales.
Monthly sales fluctuate, with certain months showing stronger revenue.
